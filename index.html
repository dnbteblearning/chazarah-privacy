Privacy Policy

Chazarah Tracker — Last updated: February 19, 2026

In short: Chazarah Tracker works fully offline by default. If you choose to sign in, we store only what is needed to sync your data and enable group features. We never sell your data or use third-party analytics.

Offline by Default

Chazarah Tracker works entirely offline without an account. All your learning progress, review schedules, tracks, and settings are stored locally on your device. You are never required to sign in.

Optional Account & Cloud Sync

If you choose to create an account (via email/password, Google Sign-In, or Apple Sign-In), we collect and store:

  • Email address — used for authentication and account recovery
  • Authentication provider — which sign-in method you used
  • Learning data — your tracks, schedules, and review history are synced to the cloud so you can back up and restore your data

This data is stored securely using Supabase (hosted on Amazon Web Services). We do not store your password directly — authentication is handled securely by Supabase Auth.

Groups (Chaburos)

If you join or create a group, the following additional data is shared with other group members:

  • Your display name
  • Your learning progress within the group

Group data is visible only to members of that group.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using an on-device database. If you sign in, a copy of your data is also synced to our cloud database for backup and cross-device access. You can use the app without an account, and your local data remains on your device.

Third-Party Services

If you sign in, the app uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — authentication, database, and real-time sync
  • Google Sign-In (Android) — if you choose to sign in with Google
  • Apple Sign-In (iOS) — if you choose to sign in with Apple

We do not use any advertising networks, analytics tools, or tracking technologies.

Permissions

The app may request permission to send local notifications to remind you of upcoming reviews. No data is sent externally as part of this feature.

Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated cloud data at any time from the app settings. Local data on your device can be removed by uninstalling the app.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app can be used fully offline without an account, which requires no personal information.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be posted on this page with a revised date.
